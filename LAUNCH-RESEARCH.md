## 1. Naming & Branding

### The Problem
"Thumos" (ancient Egyptian afterlife) is already used by another AI app. We need something unique, SEO-friendly, and representative.

### Top 5 Recommendations

| Rank | Name | Origin | Why It Works | SEO Risk |
|------|------|--------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | **Thumos** | Greek — the spirited part of Plato's tripartite soul | Short, punchy, uncontested. Thumos is the spirited self — passion, identity, values. 2 syllables. | Very low |
| 2 | **Ennoia** | Greek — "inner thought, understanding" | Elegant, feminine, perfect meaning. Literally "what is within the mind." Pronounced en-NOY-ah. | Very low |
| 3 | **Soulscope** | English compound | Immediately communicable. "A scope for your soul." Everyone gets it. Easy to spell/say. | Very low |
| 4 | **Nosce** | Latin — from "nosce te ipsum" (know thyself) | The Delphi inscription + The Matrix reference. Short, strong. Tagline: "Nosce — know thyself." | Low |
| 5 | **Nephesh** | Hebrew — "whole living being, breath, self" | Holistic soul concept (not just mind). Cross-cultural. Uncontested commercially. | Very low |

**Honorable mentions:** Soulweave (the weaving metaphor fits synthesis well), Entelechy (Aristotle's "realization of potential" — the most philosophically precise but 4 syllables), Khora (Plato's "receptacle for becoming").

### Brand Positioning

The name should support this positioning:
- **Not therapy.** Not wellness. Not journaling. Not a chatbot companion.
- **A soul mirror.** An AI that helps you understand who you really are, through conversation.
- **Taglines by name:**
  - Thumos: "The spirit within"
  - Ennoia: "What lives within your mind"
  - Soulscope: "See yourself clearly"
  - Nosce: "Know thyself"

## 2. Soul Dashboard Design

### Personality System: "Soul Spectrum" (Custom, Not MBTI)

**Why not MBTI/Enneagram:** The app's core principle (soul.ts line 48) says *"No labels. Never say 'you are an INTJ.'"* Using standard personality types would violate this.

**The approach:** Use Big Five psychology as the hidden engine, but present it through the app's own poetic language. The dual soul file architecture already supports this — HiddenSoulFile stores clinical data with numeric `strength` values (0-1 scale), while VisibleSoulFile presents everything as prose.

**7 Soul Spectrum Dimensions:**

| Dimension | Big Five Mapping | What It Captures |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Curiosity Horizon | Openness | How wide vs. deep they explore |
| Rhythm & Structure | Conscientiousness | How they organize their life |
| Energy Direction | Extraversion | Inward vs. outward energy |
| Heart Stance | Agreeableness | How they relate to others |
| Inner Weather | Neuroticism | Their emotional landscape |
| Depth Seeking | (Custom) | How readily they go beneath the surface |
| Narrative Identity | (Custom) | How strong their self-story is |

**Presentation:** Not "Openness: 82%" — instead:
> **Curiosity Horizon:** You lean toward the wide-open. Ideas pull you like gravity — you'd rather explore ten directions than master one.

Each dimension gets a poetic description that references the user's actual words.

**Soul Archetype (after 3+ sessions):** A unique 2-3 word evocative label generated by the LLM. Not "INTJ" but something like *"The Restless Cartographer"* or *"The Quiet Furnace"*. Drawn from their metaphors and patterns.

### Visualizations

**Primary: Radar Chart (Soul Fingerprint)**
- 7-axis spider chart showing all spectrum dimensions
- Gold accent on near-white background (matches existing palette)
- The shape itself becomes a visual fingerprint — "your shape"
- Built with custom SwiftUI `Shape` + `Path` (no external dependencies)
- Spring animation on appear

**Secondary: Trait Spectrum Bars**
- Horizontal position indicators on a continuum (not progress bars — avoids "more is better" implication)
- Each end labeled with the poles in plain language:
  ```
  Curiosity Horizon
  [deep focus -------|------------ wide exploration]
                     ^
  ```

**Tertiary: Evolution Timeline**
- Minimal timeline showing how the soul file changed across sessions
- Uses existing `version` + `lastUpdated` tracking
- Tap a point to see what changed

### Implementation Path

| Phase | Work | Effort |
|-------|------|--------|
| A | Add `soulSpectrum` to schemas + synthesis prompt (backend) | 1-2 days |
| B | Radar chart + spectrum display (iOS) | 2-3 days |
| C | Evolution timeline | 1 day |
| D | Soul archetype generation | 1 day |

### Key Files to Modify
- `src/domain/schemas.ts` — add `soulSpectrumSchema`
- `src/domain/soulFile.ts` — update synthesis prompt to output 7 continuous scores + archetype
- `Thumos/App/Models.swift` — add `SoulSpectrum` struct
- `Thumos/App/SoulFileScreen.swift` — add radar chart, spectrum bars, archetype

## 3. Share Feature

### Three Tiers of Shareable Content

**Tier 1 — Soul Card (low intimacy, high shareability):**
- Soul archetype name
- Portrait text (2-4 sentences)
- Radar chart shape (visual fingerprint)
- App branding
- This is the "Spotify Wrapped" equivalent — visually striking, conversation-starting

**Tier 2 — Insight Card (medium intimacy):**
- A single soul section or crystallized moment as a beautiful quote card
- User explicitly chooses which insight to share

**Tier 3 — Full Soul File (high intimacy):**
- PDF or time-limited deep link (expires in 24-48 hours)
- Feels like sharing a letter, not a social post

### Privacy Principles
- Nothing shares automatically — every share requires explicit user choice
- Granular control — user picks exactly what to share
- Share preview before sending
- Use system share sheet (app doesn't know who they share with)
- "Discovered through [App Name]" branding, no user identifier unless they add it

### Technical Implementation
- `ImageRenderer` (iOS 16+) to render SwiftUI views as shareable images
- Native `ShareLink` for the share sheet
- Soul card designed at 390pt fixed width for consistency
- ~1-2 days of work

### Key Apple Review Risks for AI Apps

1. **Guideline 5.1.2(i) — Third-Party AI Data Sharing** — The #1 rejection reason for AI apps in 2025-2026. You MUST have an explicit consent modal naming Anthropic/Claude before any data is sent.

2. **Guideline 4.2 — Minimum Functionality** — Apple rejects "thin wrappers." Mitigated by: native SwiftUI, Keychain storage, SSE streaming, offline caching.

3. **Guideline 1.2 — User-Generated Content** — Need content reporting mechanism.

4. **AI disclaimers** — Don't claim therapeutic or diagnostic capabilities. Include: "This is not a psychological evaluation or medical advice."

## 6. Marketing Strategy

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What They Do | How We're Different |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Replika | AI companion/friend | We're a mirror, not a friend. No role-playing. |
| Pi (Inflection) | Conversational AI with personality | We build a persistent soul file that evolves. |
| Character.AI | Role-playing chatbot | We reflect the user's identity, not fictional characters. |
| Dimensional | Personality test app | We discover personality through conversation, not questionnaires. |

**Our unique position:** The only app that builds a living, evolving portrait of who you are through reflective conversation — not a chatbot, not a quiz, not therapy.

### Pre-Launch Timeline

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Register domain, deploy website + privacy policy |
| Week 2 | Build AI consent modal, data deletion, content reporting |
| Week 3 | Internal TestFlight (friends/family, 10-20 people) |
| Week 4 | External TestFlight beta (wider audience, ~100) |
| Week 5 | App Store screenshots, description, keywords |
| Week 6 | Submit for App Store review |

### Marketing Channels (Budget: $0-100)

**Highest ROI — Free:**

1. **Reddit** — Post in r/artificial, r/selfimprovement, r/psychology, r/productivity. Genuine posts about the journey of building a soul mirror, not ads. Reddit hates marketing but loves authentic builder stories.

2. **Twitter/X** — Build in public. Share the philosophy behind the app, not just features. Thread: "I built an AI that doesn't give advice — it just mirrors who you are. Here's why."

3. **Hacker News** — "Show HN" post. Frame it as a technical + philosophical project. HN loves novel AI applications with genuine depth.

4. **Product Hunt** — Launch day coordination. Target a Tuesday or Wednesday. Prepare a compelling tagline, screenshots, and a short demo video.

5. **IndieHackers** — Share the journey, revenue numbers (when available), technical decisions.

**Medium ROI — Paid ($50-100):**

6. **Apple Search Ads** — $50 test budget on keywords like "self-reflection app", "personality AI", "know yourself." Highly targeted, intent-based.

### App Store Optimization (ASO)

**Target keywords** (in 100-char limit):
```
personality,soul,self-discovery,AI reflection,journal,identity,self-awareness,introspection,mirror
```

**Description hook** (first 3 lines visible before "Read More"):
> [App Name] is a soul mirror — an AI that helps you understand who you really are through honest, reflective conversation. No quizzes. No labels. Just a mirror.

### TestFlight Beta Strategy

| Wave | Size | Duration | Goal |
|------|------|----------|------|

| External Wave 1 | 50-100 | 1 week | Validate core experience, collect testimonials |
