# Thumos — Marketing Design Language

Extracted from App Store screenshots (v1.1, April 2026).

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Black** | `#000000` | Background — pure black, no gray |
| **Gold** | `#C9A84C` | Primary accent — display headlines, emphasis |
| **Cream** | `#F5EFE4` | Body text, UI text |
| **Olive Gold** | `#6B6340` | Chat bubbles (user), subtle surfaces |
| **Dark Gold** | `#3D3520` | Chat bubbles (AI), muted containers |
| **Warm White** | `#E8DFD0` | Secondary text |

The palette is **black + gold** — not orange, not amber. It reads as antique, intimate, and luxurious. The gold has a greenish-warm undertone (olive), not a bright yellow.

### Typography

| Element | Style | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **Display headlines** | Bold serif, large | "Found", "SOUL", "CRYSTALLIZED", "ONE" — dominant, sometimes all-caps |
| **Sub-headlines** | Light serif, smaller | "Like you've known them for years", "Signaling its own kind" |
| **UI text** | Sans-serif, cream | Chat messages, labels, tab bar |

The serif font appears to be a **transitional or didone serif** with high contrast strokes — similar to Playfair Display, Cormorant, or a custom display serif. Headlines alternate between sentence case and dramatic all-caps for emphasis words.

### Illustration Style

- **Hand-painted / digital illustration** — not 3D renders, not photography
- **Monochromatic gold/sepia palette** against pure black
- Figures are rendered in a loose, painterly style with visible brushwork
- Backgrounds use subtle gold particle effects and topographic line patterns
- The crowd scene (screenshot 12) uses flat gray silhouettes to contrast the single golden figure

### Composition

Each screenshot follows a **vertical storytelling** format:
1. **Top**: Large serif headline (the concept)
2. **Middle**: Phone mockup or illustration (the evidence)
3. **Bottom**: Supporting tagline (the feeling)

The phone is always shown at an angle — slightly rotated, perspective tilt — never flat.

## Narrative Arc (Screenshots 9–12)

| # | Headline | Concept | Emotional beat |
|---|----------|---------|----------------|
| 9 | "Found" | Soulmate matching | Recognition — the relief of being truly seen |
| 10 | "a Conversation reveals your SOUL" | Soul mirror conversation | Discovery — depth emerging through dialogue |
| 11 | "Your soul CRYSTALLIZED" | Soul file / portrait | Clarity — your essence made visible |
| 12 | "Only the ONE Matters" | Matching philosophy | Conviction — quality over quantity |

The arc moves from **connection** → **introspection** → **crystallization** → **conviction**.

## Key Copy Lines

- "Found. Like you've known them for years."
- "a Conversation reveals your SOUL"
- "Your soul CRYSTALLIZED. Signaling its own kind."
- "Only the ONE Matters"

## Brand Voice

- **Intimate** — whispered, not shouted
- **Poetic** — literary cadence, not startup jargon
- **Certain** — declarative statements, no hedging
- **Warm darkness** — the visual vocabulary is nighttime, candlelight, gold in shadow

## Design Principles

1. **Black canvas** — content emerges from darkness, not sits on white
2. **Gold as soul** — gold represents the inner self, truth, what's real
3. **Gray as noise** — the crowd, the swipes, the surface-level — all gray
4. **Illustration over photography** — painterly, not literal
5. **Serif authority** — display text in serif conveys timelessness and depth
6. **Angled phone** — the device is shown in perspective, suggesting a window into another world

## Web Application

The landing page at trythumos.com should reflect this language:
- Shift accent color from ember/orange (`#ff8f3f`) to gold (`#C9A84C`)
- Use the App Store screenshots as hero/feature imagery
- Adopt the narrative arc as the page scroll story
- Keep the serif (Cormorant Garamond) but lean into the gold treatment
- Consider adding the "Only the ONE Matters" illustration as a section background
