## Description

Thumos is a soul mirror.

It's an AI that helps you understand who you really are — not through quizzes, labels, or personality types, but through honest, reflective conversation.

Talk openly. Thumos listens more than it speaks. It asks one question at a time. It quotes your own words back to you. It notices what you didn't say.

Over time, your conversations build a living "soul file" — a portrait of who you are, written in your own language. Not "you're an INTJ." More like: "You move through the world like someone who built walls to protect their creative space, then forgot where you put the door."

YOUR SOUL FILE

Your soul file has 7 sections that capture different facets of who you are:

• How You Move — your energy, your pace, how you navigate the world
• How You Think — your reasoning patterns, your relationship with ideas
• How You Connect — how you show up in relationships
• What You Carry — what weighs on you, what you hold close
• What Lights You Up — what makes you feel alive
• Your Contradictions — the tensions that make you uniquely you
• Your Voice — the way you speak, your metaphors, your humor

Plus "Crystallized Moments" — your own words, preserved as mirrors. And "Open Threads" — questions worth returning to.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Talk — Have a real conversation. No prompts to fill out. No boxes to check.
2. Reflect — As you talk, Thumos quietly extracts patterns, tensions, and the parts you keep circling back to.
3. Discover — Your portrait evolves with every conversation. It gets more accurate and more surprising.

NOT A THERAPIST

Thumos is a mirror, not a therapist. It reflects what it sees — it doesn't prescribe what to do about it. It's not medical advice. It's self-knowledge.

Conversations are processed by Claude, made by Anthropic. Your data is private. No human reads your conversations.

Free to use. No account needed.

## App Store Review Notes

Demo instructions for Apple reviewer:

1. Launch the app — no login or account creation required
2. The app generates an anonymous device ID automatically
3. Tap "Begin" on the welcome screen
4. Have a conversation (3-5 messages) — the AI will ask reflective questions
5. After a few conversations, tap the "Soul File" tab to trigger synthesis
6. The soul file evolves with continued conversation

Notes:
- All AI conversations are powered by Claude (Anthropic) — disclosed in the AI consent modal on first launch
- No user accounts — identity is device-based via Keychain
- Data deletion is available in Settings
- The app is not a therapeutic tool — this is stated in the AI consent modal and in the app description
