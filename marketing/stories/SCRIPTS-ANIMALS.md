# Thumos Marketing Scripts — Animal Matchmaker Series

**Concept:** Animals walk into a matchmaker's office and describe what they're looking for in a partner. The comedy comes from their actual, real mating behaviors being delivered as sincere dating preferences. Two voices per video: the Animal (earnest, hopeful) and the Matchmaker (professional, increasingly disturbed).

**Format:** Conversation between ANIMAL and MATCHMAKER
**Length:** 45-75 seconds each
**Platform:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts

---

## Script 1: "The Anglerfish"

**Logline:** A tiny male anglerfish visits the matchmaker looking for a strong, independent woman. He wants commitment. REAL commitment. Like, dissolve-into-her-body commitment.

**Visual notes:** Deep sea darkness. Bioluminescent glow. Tiny male fish across the desk from a well-dressed matchmaker fish. He's small — comically small.

---

**MATCHMAKER:** So. Tell me what you're looking for.

**ANGLERFISH:** I want the real thing. I'm done playing games. I want to find her and just... become one.

**MATCHMAKER:** That's beautiful. Emotionally, you mean—

**ANGLERFISH:** No. Physically. I want to bite into her skin, fuse my face to her body, and merge our bloodstreams until my organs dissolve and I'm nothing but a small lump on her side.

**MATCHMAKER:** ...

**ANGLERFISH:** I want our circulatory systems to become one. I want to lose my eyes. My brain. My fins. Everything.

**MATCHMAKER:** Sir, that's—

**ANGLERFISH:** Just keep my testicles. That's all I need. Two functioning testicles hanging off the love of my life. Forever.

**MATCHMAKER:** So you want to be... a parasite.

**ANGLERFISH:** I want to be a HUSBAND.

**MATCHMAKER:** Is there a difference here?

**ANGLERFISH:** She can carry up to eight of us at once. I just want to be one of her eight. Is that too much to ask?

**MATCHMAKER:** Eight husbands. Fused to her body. No brains.

**ANGLERFISH:** When you say it like that it sounds weird. But when you're two miles deep in the ocean and it's pitch black and you finally find someone — you hold on. Literally. With your teeth. Until your jaw dissolves.

**MATCHMAKER:** I'm going to be honest. I don't think I can help you.

**ANGLERFISH:** That's what the last seven matchmakers said. Right before they fused to their clients.

*(beat)*

**[TEXT ON SCREEN]:** *Real fact: Male anglerfish permanently fuse into females, losing all organs except their testes. Females can carry up to 8 parasitic males at once.*

**[END CARD]:** *Finding your person shouldn't cost you your brain. — Thumos*

## Script 2: "The Praying Mantis"

**Logline:** A female praying mantis visits the matchmaker. She seems lovely and reasonable — at first. She wants a good dancer. Fit. Passionate. She just has one tiny post-date ritual she'd like to discuss.

**Visual notes:** Elegant office. The mantis is well-dressed, poised, legs crossed. Big green eyes. She's charming. The matchmaker is sweating.

---

**MATCHMAKER:** Tell me about your ideal partner.

**MANTIS:** Tall. Good dancer — that's non-negotiable. I need a man who can move. The little shimmy, the body sway? I notice that.

**MATCHMAKER:** Love that. What else?

**MANTIS:** Confident. Not afraid to make the first move. But respectful, you know? He should approach slowly. Carefully.

**MATCHMAKER:** Great instincts. And what are you looking for long-term?

**MANTIS:** Oh, I wouldn't say long-term.

**MATCHMAKER:** Something more casual?

**MANTIS:** Something more... one-time.

**MATCHMAKER:** A one-night stand?

**MANTIS:** A one-night meal.

**MATCHMAKER:** ...come again?

**MANTIS:** During. I eat his head during. It actually helps him finish — the body keeps going longer without the brain. It's a win-win.

**MATCHMAKER:** In what universe is that a win for him?

**MANTIS:** He gets to reproduce AND he feeds his children. Through me. He's literally inside me forever. That's romance.

**MATCHMAKER:** That's a crime scene.

**MANTIS:** Look — I've been on a lot of dates. And the ones who survive? They just don't have that spark. If I don't want to eat you, I'm not that into you.

**MATCHMAKER:** How many... dates have you been on?

**MANTIS:** This season? Fourteen.

**MATCHMAKER:** And how many of them are still...

**MANTIS:** Alive? ...Next question.

*(beat)*

**MATCHMAKER:** I'm going to put you in our "advanced" category.

**MANTIS:** I get that a lot.

*(beat)*

**[TEXT ON SCREEN]:** *Real fact: Female praying mantises bite off the male's head during mating. The headless body continues mating — sometimes more vigorously.*

**[END CARD]:** *Love shouldn't be a death sentence. — Thumos*

## Script 3: "The Hippo"

**Logline:** A male hippo walks into the matchmaker's office absolutely sure he's a catch. Three tons of confidence. His signature move? Explosive poop-spraying via helicopter tail. He cannot understand why he's single.

**Visual notes:** The hippo barely fits in the chair. Big, confident energy. Leaning back. The matchmaker's desk is tiny by comparison. There's water damage on the walls already.

---

**MATCHMAKER:** So what do you bring to a relationship?

**HIPPO:** Everything. I'm three tons. I'm territorial. I own a whole stretch of river. Prime real estate.

**MATCHMAKER:** Great. And what's your approach when you see someone you like?

**HIPPO:** OK so — I turn around. I lift my tail. And I spin it. Like a helicopter blade.

**MATCHMAKER:** A helicopter blade?

**HIPPO:** Yeah, real fast. Maximum RPM. And while I'm spinning—

**MATCHMAKER:** Oh no.

**HIPPO:** I spray. Everywhere. Full three-sixty coverage. She will be COVERED.

**MATCHMAKER:** Covered in—

**HIPPO:** My love.

**MATCHMAKER:** That's not love, sir. That's feces.

**HIPPO:** It's a DISPLAY. It shows her what I've been eating. It says "look at my diet, look at my territory, look at my output." It's basically a résumé.

**MATCHMAKER:** A résumé made of—

**HIPPO:** Made of ME. Raw. Unfiltered. Authentic.

**MATCHMAKER:** Have you considered... flowers?

**HIPPO:** Flowers? FLOWERS? I'm not a flamingo. I'm a three-ton apex river beast. When I show up, things get wet and things get real. That's my brand.

**MATCHMAKER:** And how do the ladies respond to this... brand?

**HIPPO:** Mixed reviews. But I think that's because they haven't experienced the full spray radius. I've been working on my distance.

**MATCHMAKER:** I'm sorry, your distance?

**HIPPO:** Used to be six feet. Now I can hit twelve. Easy. On a good day — fifteen.

**MATCHMAKER:** I need you to stop improving at this.

**HIPPO:** You sound just like my last matchmaker. Before I sprayed her office.

**MATCHMAKER:** Is that why the walls are—

**HIPPO:** That was here when I got here.

*(beat)*

**[TEXT ON SCREEN]:** *Real fact: Male hippos attract mates by defecating while rapidly spinning their tails like propellers, flinging feces up to 15 feet in every direction.*

**[END CARD]:** *First impressions matter. Make yours count. — Thumos*

## Production Notes

- **Two voices needed:** Matchmaker (calm, professional, dry humor — same voice across all 3) + Animal (different voice per animal, earnest and sincere)
- **Matchmaker voice:** Think deadpan therapist. No laughing. The comedy comes from the professional tone breaking.
- **Anglerfish voice:** Small, eager, slightly desperate. Puppy energy. A simp.
- **Mantis voice:** Elegant, feminine, seductive. Think femme fatale who doesn't understand why everyone is horrified.
- **Hippo voice:** Deep, booming, alpha-male confidence. Zero self-awareness. Gym bro energy.
- **Visual style:** Animated or AI-generated animals in a cozy matchmaker's office. Anthropomorphic but still clearly animals. Pixar-adjacent vibe.
- **Pacing:** Let the jokes breathe. Pauses after punchlines. The matchmaker's silence IS the joke.
- **Music:** Soft romantic muzak playing in the matchmaker's office throughout. Doesn't change even when things get horrifying.
- **Fact cards:** Real scientific facts at the end ground the absurdity in truth — that's the viral hook.
