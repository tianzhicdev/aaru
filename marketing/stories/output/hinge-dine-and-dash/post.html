I (30f) went on my first date in 6 years and I am NEVER dating again

So I was in a relationship for 5 years. Ended earlier this year and on Friday I was lonely had no plans on Halloween and decided to download Hinge (first time user).

Was shocked I got 100+ likes the first day and got overwhelmed. I also was shocked a few men already asked me out within two days.

So one guy who was pretty attractive and lives in my town asked me out and we had a good text convo on Friday and Saturday so I said yes. Honestly I wouldn't usually say yes so quick but I want to get out there and practice so I had low expectations.

He tells me he will pick me up after work to take me to a restaurant (more on the pricey side his choice). I said I will drive there — and this should have been the first red flag 🚩 He kept insisting on picking me up because he's a 'true gentleman.' I doubled down and he said ok see you there.

Well we met up. I was nervous but he looked just like his photos and was very friendly, bit over the top (pushed my chair in). He was already there and had ordered us drinks. I didn't plan to drink but I figured because he already ordered I should just take it.

We end up talking mostly about what we are looking for and our jobs. He gets vulnerable, says he truly wants love, says he feels something different about me versus other women. Honestly I wanted it to be true but I have always been a pretty skeptical person.

We are there an hour. He ordered another drink and two appetizers and an entree. I just ordered my entree. I denied the second drink but he kept insisting. He ordered it anyway but I didn't drink it so HE had to drink it.

He laughed and said he had to use the bathroom since he drank so much. I had a weird feeling at that moment like 'oh don't tell me…'

Well I waited about 5 minutes. Waitress came to check on me. I told her I was on a first date — not gonna lie the tears started flowing a bit because I was so embarrassed that I likely got left with the bill, and that the guy ordered all this stuff and left me with the bill. I told her I only got the entree.

I gave the manager his name and showed his photo. They said they will split the check to just include my entree, and at this point it's been 15 minutes so it was very clear he left.

Just want to say that I'm never in my life going on another first date off an app because what the actual fuck was that.
