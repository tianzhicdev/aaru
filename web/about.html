How Magpie Works

Magpie is an AI that learns who you are through conversation, then finds the person who fits. No swiping. No photos. Just understanding.

1. You talk to Magpie

Magpie asks questions. Not quiz questions. Real ones. About how you love, what makes you pull away, what a good day actually looks like, and what you need from someone but have never said out loud.

There's no time limit. No questionnaire. Just conversation that gets deeper the more you come back.

2. Magpie builds your portrait

As you talk, Magpie listens for patterns. Not just what you say, but how you say it. What you avoid. What lights you up. What contradicts what you said last time.

It builds a living portrait that covers:

  • Attachment style — how you bond, what makes you feel secure
  • Love language — how you give and receive love
  • Conflict and repair — how you handle disagreements
  • Values and worldview — what you believe in
  • Vulnerability and trust — how you open up
  • Partnership vision — what your ideal relationship looks like

The portrait evolves with every conversation. It gets richer and more accurate over time. You can read it, explore it, and learn from it.

3. Magpie carries the thread between two people

Once your portrait has enough depth, Magpie evaluates compatibility with other users across romance dimensions. Not surface preferences. Deep fit:

  • Attachment fit — do your bonding styles complement each other?
  • Conflict compatibility — can you navigate disagreements together?
  • Love language resonance — do you speak each other's language?
  • Values alignment — do you share a vision for life?
  • Emotional depth match — do you meet each other at the same level?

4. When two people fit, Magpie tells you why

Each match comes with a story. Not a percentage. A specific, personalized explanation of how your patterns resonate with theirs.

When you message your match, you already know why you're compatible. The conversation starts from depth, not from a photo.

Privacy by default

Your portrait is never shared with other users. Matches see only your display name and a compatibility story. Your full portrait, conversation history, and analysis remain completely private.
