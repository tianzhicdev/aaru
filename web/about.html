About

What Is Thumos?

Thumos is a dating app that uses AI conversations to build a living portrait of who you are, then matches you with deeply compatible people. No swiping. No photos. Just understanding.

The Problem with Dating Apps

Dating apps are broken. Swiping on photos tells you nothing about compatibility. Questionnaires capture what people think they want, not who they actually are. 78% of dating app users report burnout. The average user spends 45 minutes a day swiping and comes away feeling more exhausted than hopeful.

The fundamental issue: existing apps optimize for engagement, not for connection. More swipes, more time on the app, more revenue. But more swipes don't lead to better matches. They lead to fatigue.

How Thumos Works

Thumos takes a fundamentally different approach. Instead of swiping, you have reflective conversations with an AI that acts as a warm, perceptive friend. Through natural conversation, Thumos builds a deep psychological portrait — a "soul file" — that captures who you really are in relationships.

This portrait covers dimensions that matter for lasting compatibility:

  • Attachment style — how you bond, what makes you feel secure
  • Love language — how you give and receive love
  • Conflict and repair — how you handle disagreements and reconnect
  • Values and worldview — what you believe in and care about
  • Vulnerability and trust — how you open up and what you protect
  • Partnership vision — what your ideal relationship looks like
  • Daily rhythm and play — how you spend your time and find joy

The portrait evolves with every conversation. It gets richer and more accurate over time, capturing nuances that no questionnaire could.

Soul Mirror: AI-Powered Self-Discovery

The Soul Mirror is Thumos's core feature. It's a reflective AI conversation that helps you understand yourself — your patterns, your contradictions, your blind spots, and what you actually need in a partner (which is often different from what you think you want).

This isn't a chatbot that gives dating advice. It's a mirror. It listens for patterns in your words, your metaphors, your hesitations, and what you avoid. Then it synthesizes everything into a living portrait that you can see and explore.

Many users find the self-discovery aspect valuable even before matching. Understanding your attachment style, conflict patterns, and love language is powerful on its own.

Matching: Compatibility Across Romance Dimensions

Once your portrait reaches sufficient depth, Thumos evaluates compatibility with other users across romance dimensions. The matching engine doesn't just look at surface preferences — it evaluates deep compatibility:

  • Attachment fit — do your bonding styles complement each other?
  • Conflict compatibility — can you navigate disagreements together?
  • Love language resonance — do you naturally speak each other's language?
  • Values alignment — do you share a vision for life?
  • Emotional depth match — do you meet each other at the same level?

Each match comes with a personalized explanation — not a percentage, but a story of why your souls resonate. You'll understand exactly why Thumos thinks you connect.

Privacy-First Design

Unlike every other dating app, Thumos never shows your profile or photos to other users. When you match with someone, they see only your display name and an AI-generated compatibility summary. Your full portrait, conversation history, and psychological analysis remain completely private.

This isn't just a feature — it's a philosophy. Real connection starts with understanding, not appearance. When you message your match, you already know why you're compatible. The conversation starts from depth, not from a photo.

Who Is Thumos For?

Thumos is for people who are tired of swiping and want something deeper. People who believe compatibility is about more than photos. People who are curious about themselves and open to a different approach to finding love.

If any of these describe you, Thumos might be what you've been looking for:

  • You're exhausted by dating app burnout and the endless swipe cycle
  • You want matches based on who you are, not how you look
  • You're interested in self-discovery and personal growth
  • You value privacy and don't want your photos shown to strangers
  • You believe real compatibility goes deeper than a bio and six photos

The Technology

Thumos is powered by Claude, made by Anthropic — one of the most capable AI systems in the world. Conversations use Claude Opus for deep understanding. Soul file synthesis uses models tuned for psychological reflection and extraction.

The matching engine uses LLM-based evaluation with structured output — not simple keyword matching or collaborative filtering. It reads and understands both users' soul portraits, then evaluates compatibility across multiple romance dimensions simultaneously.
