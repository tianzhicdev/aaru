How Thumos Works — A Dating App That Understands You

Thumos is a dating app that uses AI conversations to build a living portrait of who you are, then matches you with deeply compatible people. No swiping. No photos. Just understanding.

Soul Mirror: AI-Powered Self-Discovery

Instead of filling out a questionnaire or swiping on photos, you have reflective conversations with an AI that listens deeply. Through natural conversation, Thumos builds a rich psychological portrait covering your attachment style, love language, values, conflict approach, emotional depth, and partnership vision. The portrait evolves and gets more accurate with every conversation.

Compatibility Matching Across Romance Dimensions

Once your portrait reaches sufficient depth, Thumos evaluates compatibility with other users across romance dimensions: attachment fit, conflict compatibility, love language resonance, shared values, and partnership vision alignment. Each match includes a personalized explanation of why you connect — not a percentage, but a story.

Privacy-First: Your Portrait Is Never Shared

Unlike Tinder, Hinge, or Bumble, Thumos never shows your profile or photos to other users. Matches only see your display name and an AI-generated compatibility summary. Your full portrait, conversation history, and psychological analysis remain completely private.

Why People Choose Thumos Over Other Dating Apps

  • No swiping — AI conversations replace the swipe-based matching model
  • Personality-based matching — compatibility evaluated across romance dimensions, not photos
  • Self-discovery built in — understand yourself before finding your person
  • Private by default — your portrait is never shared with matches
  • Free to use on iPhone
