Support

THUMOS Support and FAQ

If you need help with Thumos, email support@trythumos.com.

This is not crisis support

Thumos is not a therapist, medical service, or emergency service. If you are in crisis, call or text 988 in the United States or contact a local emergency or mental health professional.

What is Thumos?

Thumos is not a dating app. It is a private AI soul mirror that helps you understand who you really are through honest, reflective conversation. Over time, it builds a living portrait called your soul file.

Is this therapy?

No. Thumos is not a therapist and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is a reflective tool - a mirror, not a prescription.

Who sees my conversations?

Your conversations are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to generate responses and synthesis. Thumos is designed so your data stays private to you. No advertising use or sale of your data is part of the product.

How does the soul file work?

As you talk, Thumos listens for patterns - your words, your metaphors, your contradictions, and what you avoid. It quietly builds a running model of you during conversation. When you're ready, a deeper synthesis distills everything into a living portrait that gets richer over time.

What AI powers Thumos?

Thumos uses Claude, made by Anthropic. Conversation responses use Claude Opus, and soul-file synthesis uses Claude models tuned for reflection and extraction.

How do I delete my data?

In the app, open Settings and choose Delete My Data. This permanently deletes your conversations, soul file, and device identity. You can also email support@trythumos.com.

Is Thumos free?

Yes. Thumos is free to use.

Where can I get the app?

Thumos is available for iPhone on the App Store. Free, no account needed.
